Url Shortener

To get json, append desired url to end of '/new' path(must include 'http://' or 'https://'.

example:

https://fccback-url-short.herokuapp.com/new/https://google.com

will return:

{url: https://google.com, short_url: https://fccback-url-short.herokuapp.com/s/1}

shortened url will redirect to previously entered site.
